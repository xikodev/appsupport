# Pixel Library Support

Last updated: June 20, 2026

## Contact

- Support email: `xikodevapps@gmail.com`
- Privacy policy: `https://appsupport.xikodev.xyz/pixel-library/privacy-policy/`
- Account deletion page: `https://appsupport.xikodev.xyz/pixel-library/account-deletion/`

## Support Scope

- Account access issues
- Login and signup problems
- Session tracking bugs
- Group invite and membership issues
- Privacy questions
- Account deletion requests

## Response Targets

- Account access issues: 2 business days
- Bug reports: 5 business days
- Privacy or deletion requests: 30 days or faster if required by law

## Account Deletion

- Users can delete their account in the app from the profile screen.
- Users who cannot access the app can request deletion through the web deletion page.

## Information To Include In Requests

- Username
- Email address on the account
- Device platform and app version, if reporting a bug
- Clear reproduction steps, if reporting a bug

## Abuse And Safety

- Authentication abuse protection may temporarily rate limit repeated failed login attempts.
- Group management actions are restricted by group roles and ownership rules.
