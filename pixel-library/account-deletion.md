# Pixel Library Account Deletion

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Pixel Library supports account deletion both inside the app and through a web-accessible support path.

## In-App Deletion

1. Sign in to Pixel Library.
2. Open `Profile`.
3. Tap `Delete profile`.
4. Confirm the deletion request.

## Web Deletion Request

If you cannot access the app, request deletion through:

- `support@xikodev.xyz`

## What Happens When A Request Is Completed

- The Pixel Library account is deleted.
- Associated account data stored for normal app operation is deleted from active systems.
- Limited information may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, backups, or legal compliance.

## Information To Provide

- Username
- Email address used on the account
- A short statement confirming that you want the account deleted

## Response Window

- We aim to confirm account deletion requests within 30 days or faster when required by law.
